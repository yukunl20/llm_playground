#### Expanding
**Expanding** is the task of taking a shorter piece of text, such as instructions or a list of topics, and using a large language model to generate a longer piece of text, like an email or an essay. This is highly effective for using the model as a **brainstorming partner**.

Responsible Use and Transparency

- **Avoid Spam:** Expanding should only be used in a responsible way to help people, not to generate large amounts of spam.
- **AI Transparency:** When showing AI-generated text to a user, it is important to be transparent and let them know the text was created by AI.
- **Sign-offs:** For customer service tasks, the model should sign responses as an "AI customer agent".

Example: Personalized Customer Email

You can generate custom email responses based on the sentiment of a customer review. The prompt should instruct the model to use specific details from the review and maintain a concise, professional tone.

``` python
import openai

def get_completion(prompt, model="gpt-3.5-turbo", temperature=0):
    messages = [{"role": "user", "content": prompt}]
    response = openai.ChatCompletion.create(
        model=model,
        messages=messages,
        temperature=temperature, # Controls randomness
    )
    return response.choices.message["content"]

# Example prompt for expanding a review into an email
prompt = f"""
You are a customer service AI assistant.
Your task is to send an email reply to a valued customer.
Given the customer email delimited by ```, generate a reply to thank the customer for their review.
If the sentiment is positive or neutral, thank them for their review.
If the sentiment is negative, apologize and suggest that they can reach out to customer service.
Make sure to use specific details from the review.
Review: ```{customer_review}```
Review sentiment: {sentiment}
"""
```

#### Temperature

**Temperature** is a parameter that allows you to vary the degree of exploration and variety in the model's responses.

- **Temperature = 0:** The model always chooses the most likely next word, resulting in a predictable and reliable response. This is recommended for building systems where consistency is key.
- **Higher Temperature (e.g., 0.7):** The model will occasionally choose less likely words, leading to more creative, varied, and random outputs. At higher temperatures, the assistant can be thought of as more "distractible" but more creative.

``` python
# To get a more creative or varied response, increase the temperature
response = get_completion(prompt, temperature=0.7)
```
